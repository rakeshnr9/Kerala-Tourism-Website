"God's Own Country"

Kerala, India

Famously known as 'God's Own Country', Kerala is a state located on the Malabar coast of Southern India.

Home to a vast network of brackish lagoons called backwaters, ayurvedic massages,

wildlife sanctuaries, sprawling tea gardens, hill-stations,

palm tree-lined beaches and colourful dance forms,

Kerala has everything on offer to take your pick from!
